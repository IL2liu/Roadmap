TCGA Dashboard

This page contains reported figures, generated using the TCGA Roadmap and demonstrating the reproducability of analysis therewith enabled.

Source SPARQL Endpoint

Total Number of Files in the TCGA By Date

Logarithmic progression of the number of files available to the public in the TCGA, counted using the SPARQL endpoint here reported, showing a sustained doubling every 7 months since March, 2010 and now over 1/4 million individual files in the public domain. The query used to generate the results may be accessed the SPARQL Endpoint.

Connections between Diseases and Data Types in the TCGA

Results of querying the TCGA contents for relationships between data types and disease studies, shown here as a bipartite graph. In this figure, lines between disease studies (on the left) and data type (on the right) indicate that the disease study contains files of the linked data type.

Connections between Diseases and Platforms in the TCGA

Results of querying the TCGA contents for relationships between platforms and disease studies, shown here as a bipartite graph. In this figure, lines between disease studies (on the left) and data analysis platforms (on the right) indicate that the disease study contains files generated by the linked platform. The query used to retrieve this data is available at the SPARQL Endpoint.
